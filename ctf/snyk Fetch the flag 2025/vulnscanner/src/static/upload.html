How VulnScanner Works

When you upload a template, VulnScanner will:

  1. Verify the signature of the template to ensure its authenticity.
  2. Execute the defined requests and check if they meet the specified conditions (status codes, headers, etc.).
  3. If applicable, execute the defined code block based on the results of the checks.

This ensures that only valid, trusted templates are processed and prevents the execution of unauthorized code.
